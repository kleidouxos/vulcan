“THE PLANET VULCAN”

The Invisible or Spiritual Planet
By Prof. H. L. Weston

This little book opens a new channel for investi-
gation to those who are interested in Esoteric As-
trology. It is a treatise on the history and nature of
the mysterious planet, Vulcan, with reports of ob-
servations. It has tables showing how to locate its
geocentric and heliocentric place in the Zodiac on any
date, or where it was at your birth. Price 50 cents.
