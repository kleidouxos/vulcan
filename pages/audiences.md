## Scholarly Audiences: Toward a Vocabulary

Purpose of collection: bring these diverse audiences together, fields that rarely merge, subjects historians of science tend not to focus on.

Intro: introduced vocabulary for audiences
Here defined

### Academic

scholars, primarily astronomers but also general science and philosophers of science. 
Generally assumes culture of scholarship.

Link to academic works

### Student

Specifically for self-identified textbooks - e.g., introductions
Works meant to educate, enculturate 

Link to academic works

### Popular

Primarily newspapers, magazines, and general interest books.
Reporting
Humor
But also informal debates, advertisements, self-promotion

Link to academic works

### Esoteric

Interest in western esotericism
In this collection, primarily astrology or adjacent fields
Crossing of many lines
Often as encircled as scholarship, a dense culture
But may also seek to educate 
Esotericism and science relationship stretching all the way backwards
